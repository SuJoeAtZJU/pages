About PINMOO

Since 2013, our goal has been simple: make a better life for young people, we are forever persistent in designing products with the specific needs of our partners and customers in mind, with 9 years of development, we have developed into a multifunctional company with 250 people. With warehouses in Yiwu and Anhui, our products are popular in Online and Offline Stores, currently sold in countries like the United States, Canada, Germany, Spain, Italy, the United Kingdom, and more. We have professional and dedicated teams with almost 10 years of experience ranging from market research to product design, manufacturing to sales, we have over 20 people design team and keep the design new product every month to help our clients increase sales and capture more market.
